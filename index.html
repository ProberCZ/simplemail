Welcome to the pages of SimpleMail

SimpleMail is a simple email client targeting AmigaOS and compatbile platforms. It supports the SMTP, POP3 and IMAP (in a simple form) protocols. Furthermore, among other features, it is capable of showing simple HTML mails using the SimpleHTML engine and can identify spam mails by using a Bayesian filter.

Requirements

SimpleMail uses MUI and some further publically available MUI custom classes.

Download

SimpleMail is available for the classic AmigaOS (68k), the current versions of AmigaOS (ppc) as well as MorphOS (ppc). It has also been adapted to AROS (various architectures).

Current binaries can be obtained from the SourceForge site at http://simplemail.sourceforge.net/index.php?body=downloads.

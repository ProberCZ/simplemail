SimpleMail

This is the home of the Amiga mailer SimpleMail. ATM, SimpleMail is really simple but however it is already a useable mailer. If you have any wishes concerning features, then send a mail to Sebastian Bauer <sebauer@t-online.de> or Hynek Schlawack <hynek@schlawack.net> and we will consider it in further development.

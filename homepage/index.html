SimpleMail

Hello dear Amiga users! We wish you merry x-mas and a happy and successful new year. And we have also a little present for you out there: There is a new mailer for the Amiga in development as we thought that YAM is not enough. This is just a alpha-version so far and so it as more a teaser of what you can expect in the new year. Anyway, SimpleMail is already a fully-featured mailer but still far away from YAM. But with your support, SimpleMail can overgrow YAM very soon!

Download: simplemail.lzx (44292 Bytes)

If you have any wishes concerning features, then send a mail to Sebastian Bauer <sebauer@t-online.de> or Hynek Schlawack <hynek@schlawack.net> and we will consider it in further development.

Sorry for this rather simple page so far but we use our energy for developing and not for webdesign. If you would like to be our webmaster you'll be welcome! Note that this is only a temporay place of the homepage. The real place will be http://simplemail.sourceforge.net/. SimpleMail's project page can be found at http://sourceforge.net/projects/simplemail/.

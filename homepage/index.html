SimpleMail

This is the home of the Amiga mailer SimpleMail. ATM, SimpleMail is really simple but however it is already a useable mailer. If you have any wishes concerning features, then send a mail to Sebastian Bauer or Hynek Schlawack and we will consider it in further development.
